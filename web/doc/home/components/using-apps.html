Using APPs

APPs are the extended functionality provided by third-party developers that make the workspace very powerful. APPs can take the form of anything from spell checking to games or science simulations. Installed APPs can be found on the APP panel as shown below:

  1. Installing APPs
  2. Uninstalling APPs

Installing APPs

  • To install new APPs, click on the add button on the APP panel.
    Browse APPs store and click the install button on your preferred APP.

Un-Installing APPs

  • To uninstall an APPs, go to the APP store and navigate to the My APPs tab and click the uninstall button
